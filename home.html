GMA Network to Conduct Masterclass Series, "The Icon Series", in Luzon, Visayas, and Midanao

In an effort to empower students with valuable insights and inspiration from industry leaders, the GMA Network is all set to launch the Masterclass series across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Aptly named "The Icon Series," this initiative aims to equip students with information and knowledge, paving the way for a brighter future. Scheduled to take place on October 20, 2023, at the Main Campus in Candon City, Ilocos Sur, the Kapuso Master Class Icon Series is made possible through the generous support of the Federated Supreme Student Council. By collaborating with this esteemed organization, GMA Network strives to bring unparalleled opportunities for growth and learning to the nation's youth. During the event, students will have the unique privilege of learning directly from leaders spanning various fields. These accomplished individuals will share their expertise and experiences, providing invaluable lessons and guidance, thereby igniting the spark of inspiration within the attendees. As the GMA Network takes another significant step towards educational development, students across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao can look forward to enriching their minds and embarking on a journey of personal and professional growth. Stay tuned for further updates on this groundbreaking Masterclass series, aimed at unlocking the full potential of tomorrow's leaders.
